StudyAI Planner

AI-Powered Personal Study Planning System

Project Overview

StudyAI Planner is an intelligent study planning application that uses AI to create personalized study schedules, track learning progress, and adapt study recommendations based on individual learning patterns, course load, and performance data.

Smart Scheduling

AI-powered schedule creation based on personal learning patterns

Progress Tracking

Real-time monitoring and analytics of study performance

Adaptive Learning

Personalized recommendations and schedule adjustments

Resource Management

Intelligent organization and suggestion of study materials
